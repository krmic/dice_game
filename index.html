On a turn, a player rolls the dice repeatedly. Main goal is to accumulate as many points as possible. You can roll as many times as you want, and save your score by pressing the hold button, but if a player rolls a 1, the player's turn is over and all points that he has accumulated during this turn are gone. Rolling a 1 doesn't wipe out your entire score from previous turns, just the total earned during that particular roll. When a player reaches a total of 70 points, the game ends and that player is the winner.
